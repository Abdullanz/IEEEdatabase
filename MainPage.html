Galakse

Generationally Accumulated Library of Applicable Knowledge for Students in Engineering

About

The purpose of galakse is to give members of West virginia University's IEEE student branch access to a collection of old exams, homework assignments, and other resources via any computer or smartphone. It is kept alive with the goal that students will use this material to excel in their Copmuter Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Biometric Systems courses, along with others.
