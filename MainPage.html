Galakse

Generationally Accumulated Library of Applicable Knowledge for Students in Engineering

About

The purpose of galaksē is to give members of West Virginia University’s IEEE student branch access to a collection of old exams, homework assignments, and other resources via any computer or smartphone. It is kept alive with the goal that students will use this material to excel in their Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Biometric Systems courses, along with others. galaksē is maintained by and available only to paying members of WVU IEEE. Contact us by email to learn more.

Rules

The purpose of this website is to offer academic support to the members of this student org. With that said, please be mindful to respect the following rules:

  • Do NOT copy work directly
  • Do NOT share access to this website with non-members
  • Do NOT alter the site content without permission from an editor

Violation of the above rules may result in the termination of your WVU IEEE membership and further access to the database.
